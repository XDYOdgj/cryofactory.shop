Enhance Your Sleep Experience with the Sonive Full Size Mattress Protector

Designed for comfort, support, and durability, this mattress protector is the perfect addition to any bedroom or sleeping space. Whether you're upgrading your own mattress or looking for a practical gift, it promises a restful and rejuvenating night every time.

5-Zone Ergonomic Quilted Design for Enhanced Comfort

The Sonive Full Size Mattress Protector features a 5-zone ergonomic quilted design, offering tailored support to your body's natural contours. The denser quilting in the torso and foot zones ensures even weight distribution and prevents the filling from shifting or bunching, while the head and side zones are looser for a more relaxed feel.

Ultra Soft Microfiber Material with Breathable AirFlow Max Technology

Crafted with ultra-soft microfiber, this mattress pad delivers a luxurious touch that's gentle on your skin. The AirFlow Max technology promotes breathability, keeping your mattress cool and dry throughout the night, while also wicking away moisture to maintain a fresh sleeping environment.

Deep Pocket Construction Fits Mattresses Up to 21 Inches

With a deep pocket design of 21 inches, this protector ensures a snug fit on thicker mattresses. The built-in elastic band provides double security, preventing the pad from slipping or bunching and offering a clean, seamless look.

Durable and Easy to Care for with Machine Washable Option

The 120gsm double brushed microfiber not only adds to the softness but also enhances the longevity of the product. It's machine washable and shrink resistant, making it easy to maintain and perfect for everyday use.

Why Choose the Sonive Mattress Protector?

This ergonomic mattress pad is ideal for people seeking better sleep and comfort. Its breathable, soft, and durable design makes it suitable for all seasons and all types of sleepers. Whether you're looking for a mattress pad for a bedroom, kids' room, or even a vacation home, the Sonive protector is built to last and deliver unmatched support.
