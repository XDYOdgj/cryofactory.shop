Serta Ocean Breeze Mattress Pad Protector

Experience the perfect mix of comfort, style, and sustainability with our premium mattress pad protector. Designed for all-season use, it’s a smart choice for anyone who values a clean, cool sleeping environment.

Why Choose Serta Ocean Breeze?

Made with eco-friendly Tencel fabric, this protector offers a soft, breathable surface that's gentle on your skin and kind to the planet. The Suprelle Blue Breathe fiber fill is more than just a technical feature—it's part of a global initiative to reduce ocean-bound plastic waste.

With its innovative moisture-wicking technology, it helps to keep your mattress dry and comfortable, even in the hottest months. The infused peppermint scent adds a fresh, natural aroma that acts as a subtle deterrent against odors and bacteria, so you can enjoy a clean sleep with less washing.

Key Features

  • Sustainable Tencel Fabric – Offers eco-friendly comfort and a luxurious feel
  • Suprelle Blue Breathe Fiber Fill – Provides breathability and moisture-wicking performance
  • Elastic Bottom Edge – Ensures a secure, no-slip fit for queen size mattresses
  • Fresh for Longer – Infused with peppermint oil for natural scent and odor resistance
  • Machine Washable & Durable – Built for everyday use and easy maintenance

Perfect for Every Sleep Environment

Whether you're enjoying the warm summer nights or staying cozy during winter, the Serta Ocean Breeze Mattress Pad Protector adapts to your needs. Its breathable design ensures your mattress stays cool and comfortable, and the wrinkle-free surface gives you a fresh, premium look without the hassle.

Durable & Elegant Design

Designed to resist spills, stains, and wear, this protector is the ideal partner for your mattress. It’s not only functional but also adds a touch of elegance to your bed. With ease of installation and a secure fit, it’s simple to use and keeps your sleep space looking and feeling its best.
